[ WHO WE ARE ]

Built On Integrity.

Race General Trading Co. Ltd is a South Sudan contracting company delivering technical excellence, safety, and on-time completion across construction and oil & gas sectors.

2020 Founded

[ OUR HISTORY ]

From Day One, A Standard Set.

Race General Trading Co. Ltd was established in Juba, South Sudan by Chol Sebit with a clear mandate: build things right, finish on time, and never compromise on safety.

What began as a contracting outfit quickly grew into a company trusted by both private clients and government departments. Our management team built its reputation through strict quality control and an unwavering commitment to defined completion dates — two things the industry too often treats as optional.

That growth is a reflection of our team. Their ambition, technical discipline, and pride in the work is what has carried this company forward — and what will carry it into the next generation.

2020 Company incorporated under South Sudan Companies Act, 2012. Operations commenced in Juba, Central Equatoria.
2021 Registered as a National Oil & Gas Service Company by the Ministry of Petroleum.
2023 Joined South Sudan National Chamber of Commerce, Industry & Agriculture. Importer & exporter registration granted.
Today Ten active service lines serving construction, engineering, and oil & gas clients across South Sudan.

[ WHAT DRIVES US ]

Vision, Mission & Objective

Three commitments that shape every decision, every project, every day.

01

Our Vision

To be one of the leading companies in the region across multiple fields — a company our clients, partners, and employees are proud to be associated with.

  • Clients and partners find what completes their success, with needs anticipated and fulfilled.
  • Every person working with Race General Trading can be proud of that association.
  • Society sees us as responsible, effective, and worthy of trust.
02

Our Mission

To position Race General Trading as a company of excellent standing and high-quality output — measured by one standard: do we meet the timetable, and do we meet it well?

  • All projects executed on the basis of technical skills developed through continuous growth and improvement.
  • Highest productivity achieved through our own resources and means.
  • Quality ensured across every activity and service we deliver.
03

Our Objective

Time and safety are always our first priorities. A clean record — free of delays and free of accidents — is not an aspiration. It is the baseline.

  • Every project delivered on or before the agreed completion date.
  • All vehicles and equipment covered by the best available insurance.
  • No compromise on site safety, personnel welfare, or project integrity.

[ Our Ethics ]

A Culture Built on Integrity

"We do not tolerate any wrong behaviour — and we ask our employees to uphold the same standard."

Our reputation for adherence to the highest ethical standards in work is one of our most valuable assets. It is not a marketing position. It is the reason clients return, partners stay, and our team takes pride in the company name.

We undertake full responsibility for each piece of work we perform. Where we commit, we deliver. Where we fall short, we own it. Our commercial dealings have never been compromised and never will be.

All employees are expected to adhere to our ethical standards and to the applicable laws of the Republic of South Sudan. There are no exceptions and no grey areas.
